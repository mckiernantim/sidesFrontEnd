~>~> OUR STORY ~>~>

Sides-Ways was born out of frustration. As production assistants and assistant directors, we spent countless hours creating sides by hand - a tedious, error-prone process that often meant staying late after already long production days.

We knew there had to be a better way. By combining our production experience with modern technology, we created a tool that automates the sides creation process, saving production teams valuable time and reducing errors.

Today, Sides-Ways is used by production teams across the industry, from small independent productions to major studios. Our mission remains the same: to make the sides creation process as efficient and painless as possible.
